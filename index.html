Goods and Sales Tax Awareness

What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax that has replaced many indirect taxes in India. The GST Act was passed in the Parliament on 29th March 2017 and came into effect on 1st July 2017. GST is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition.

GST is a significant reform in the Indian tax system that aims to simplify the taxation process and make it more transparent and efficient.
